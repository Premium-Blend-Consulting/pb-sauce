Center of Excellence

One bottleneck. One solution. Four pillars.

Internal change adoption is the only thing standing between Walgreens and a fully unified enterprise content operation. The COE addresses it systematically.

Strategy & Governance

Centralized roadmap ownership, release cycle planning, playbook development and maintenance. Change management discipline baked into every deployment.

Implementation

Discovery, requirements gathering, system design, configuration and new feature launch — executed against a true product model with DevOps and Agile deployment standards.

Managed Services

User onboarding, tool administration, troubleshooting, ongoing education and training. Up to 105 hours/month keeping adoption active across all 12 functions.

Strategy & Analytics

Insights framework development, recurring and ad-hoc reporting delivery, Trust Index maintenance, and SMI support. Up to 150 hours/month turning data into decisions.
