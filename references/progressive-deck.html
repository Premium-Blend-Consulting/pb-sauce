Recommendations
Recommendation #1
Agent Nudges
Nudges leverage keyword analysis, CSAT scores, AI, and other signals to deliver the next best action directly in Care Console, assisting agents proactively during live interactions.
Keyword-Driven CSAT AI-Assisted
Recommendation #2
Intent-Based Automated Routing
Deploy intent recognition to power automated routing within Sprinklr, ensuring messages are directed to the appropriate team based on content, context, and urgency. Reduces manual triage, increases speed-to-resolution, and enhances customer satisfaction.
