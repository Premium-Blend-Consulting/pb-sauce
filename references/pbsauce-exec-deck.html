The Problem

Five problems. One tool.

⏰

Time

A polished deck takes 3-5 hours. Formatting eats more time than thinking.

🎨

Consistency

Same brand, different outputs. No two decks look like they came from the same agency.

🔃

Format Wars

Slides vs. PPT vs. Keynote vs. PDF. Every handoff breaks something.

🔧

Font Distribution

GT Walsheim and TT Hoves aren't on every machine. Brand kit becomes a suggestion.

🗂

File Chaos

Decks live in Drives, Slacks, desktops, and emails. No one knows which version is current.

3
The Vision

PB Sauce generates brand-perfect decks from text or voice, powered by AI that learns from every deck we make.

This is an internal tool. Not a revenue product — an operational one. It makes every person on this team faster at the thing we do most.

The Origin Story

From question to product in six steps.

01

The Question

Watched a PM spend 4 hours formatting a QBR that took 45 minutes to think through.

02

Claude + PPTX

PPTX is a nightmare of XML. Output looked like a government form.

03

Google Slides API

Powerful but rigid. Weeks of work for mediocre output.

04

AI Deck Builders

Gamma, Beautiful.ai, Tome — generic templates, no brand control.

05

The Insight

HTML is the rendering layer. Not PowerPoint. The Trust Score deck proved it.

06

The Decision

Two days. One person. Claude Code as architect. A full web app.

The Trust Score deck was the proof of concept. PB Sauce is the product.

16
RAG Loop

Every deck makes the next one smarter.

01

Generate

User creates a deck from text, voice, or PDF

02

Save

Deck persists to Supabase with title, client, slides

03

Chunk

Each slide converts to plain text passages

04

Embed

Google AI creates 768-dim vector fingerprints

05

Store

Vectors stored in Supabase pgvector

06

Search

Similarity search finds relevant past content

07

Inject

Past content injected as generation context

08

Generate

New deck is smarter. The loop continues.
