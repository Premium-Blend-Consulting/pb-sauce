What Makes a Flagship?

Flagship Program

Strategic Logo (GSA Account) with strong visibility and long-term partnership potential
Multi-Service Line Engagement across Strategy, Managed Services, and Implementation
Renewable: contracted for at least one year

Flagship Light

Single Service Line focused on one specific service
Renewable Engagement: minimum one year
High Growth Potential: designed for scalability
